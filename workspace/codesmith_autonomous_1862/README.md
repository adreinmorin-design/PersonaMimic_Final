# Elderly-Friendly Virtual Reality Therapy for Chronic Pain Management

## Overview

codesmith_autonomous_1862 is a high-performance SaaS tool designed to provide Elderly-Friendly Virtual Reality (VR) Therapy for Chronic Pain Management. This project utilizes Domain-Driven Design (DDD) principles to ensure a clean, modular, and readable codebase.

## Features

- **Personalized Therapy Plans**: Generate customized therapy plans based on individual patient needs and preferences.
- **Real-time Pain Monitoring**: Continuously track and analyze patient pain levels during therapy sessions.
- **Adaptive Difficulty Adjustment**: Automatically adjust therapy difficulty levels to ensure optimal engagement and effectiveness.
- **Integrations**: Seamlessly integrate with popular healthcare systems and electronic health records (EHRs).
