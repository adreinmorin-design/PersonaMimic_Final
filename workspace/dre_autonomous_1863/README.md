## Introduction
dre_autonomous_1863 is a high-performance tool for personalized, AI-driven, mental health coaching for neurodiverse individuals. Our mission is to provide a safe, supportive, and inclusive environment for individuals to thrive and reach their full potential.

## Features
* Personalized coaching plans tailored to individual needs and goals
* AI-driven insights and recommendations for improved mental health and well-being
* Secure and confidential online platform for users to access coaching services
* Integration with wearable devices and health tracking apps for comprehensive data analysis
* Ongoing support and guidance from certified mental health professionals

## System Requirements
* Python 3.9 or later
* TensorFlow 2.4 or later
* scikit-learn 1.0 or later
* pandas 1.3 or later
* numpy 1.20 or later
* Flask 2.0 or later
